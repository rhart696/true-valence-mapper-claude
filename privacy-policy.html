Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 12, 2025

Your Privacy Matters
The ProActive True Valence Mapper is designed with privacy-first principles. We collect minimal data and give you complete control over your information.

1. Overview

ProActive True Valence Mapper ("the Tool") is a professional relationship mapping application designed for workplace coaching and professional development. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data with the utmost respect for your privacy.

2. Data Collection

What We Collect

  • Device Identifier: A randomly generated ID stored locally on your device
  • Map Data: The relationship maps you create (names and trust scores)
  • Timestamps: When maps are created, updated, or accessed

What We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • IP addresses
  • Cookies or tracking pixels
  • Analytics or usage metrics
  • Third-party integrations

3. Data Storage

Local Storage (Default)

By default, all your data is stored locally in your browser's storage. This means:

  • Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly save to cloud
  • Complete privacy - only you can access your local maps
  • No account required
  • Works offline

Cloud Storage (Optional)

When you choose to save to cloud:

  • Data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure
  • Anonymous authentication - no personal details required
  • Row-Level Security ensures only you can access your maps
  • Share codes allow controlled, read-only sharing
  • You can delete cloud data at any time

4. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security:

  • Input Validation: All inputs are sanitized to prevent XSS attacks
  • Row-Level Security (RLS): Database-level isolation between users
  • Anonymous Authentication: No personal data required
  • Encrypted Connections: All cloud communication uses HTTPS
  • No Cross-User Access: Technical safeguards prevent data mixing

5. Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your maps anytime
  • Export: Download your data as JSON files
  • Delete: Remove individual maps or all data
  • Portability: Import/export between devices
  • Offline Use: Full functionality without cloud

6. Data Sharing

Your Control

  • Maps are private by default
  • Share codes provide read-only access
  • You control who gets share codes
  • Shared maps cannot be modified by viewers
  • Revoke sharing by deleting the map

We Never Share

We do not and will never:

  • Sell your data
  • Share with third parties
  • Use for advertising
  • Perform analytics on your content
  • Access your maps (technical isolation prevents this)

7. Data Retention

Local Storage

Data remains until you clear browser storage or explicitly delete it.

Cloud Storage

Data remains until you delete it. No automatic deletion or expiration.

8. Children's Privacy

This Tool is designed for professional use and is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements in privacy protection. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

10. Open Source Transparency

This tool's code is available for review, ensuring complete transparency about how your data is handled. You can verify our privacy practices directly in the source code.

11. Contact Information

Questions or Concerns?

For privacy-related questions or to report security concerns:

  • GitHub Issues: Report via the project repository
  • Purpose: Professional workplace coaching tool
  • Data Controller: You (the user) maintain full control

12. Compliance

This tool is designed with privacy by design principles and aims to comply with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Privacy by Design principles
  • Data minimization best practices
Summary: We collect minimal data, store it securely, never share it, and give you complete control. Your professional relationships remain private and under your control.
